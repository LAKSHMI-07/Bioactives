Turmeric is one of the oldest spices, having been used for over 4,000 years. It is a plant that has been used since ancient times as a therapeutic medicine in Ayurveda (Indian traditional medicine), Jamu (Indonesian traditional medicine),Chinese medicine, etc. In addition to medicinal purposes, it is also widely used in cooking spices, dyes, and cosmetics.

Curcuminoids contained in turmeric are a type of polyphenol with strong antioxidant power, and are expected to not only have well-known liver care benefits, but also cancer prevention, anti-inflammatory effects, anti-thrombotic effects, and effects on Alzheimer's disease.

Our turmeric extract uses high-quality cultivars whose origin has been certified by the USDA and the Indian government, and is fully traceable regarding cultivation areas, cultivation processes, yields, and seed quality. Processed in a specialized plant extraction facility that is ISO certified. Standardized with the highest concentration of curcuminoids in the industry. Various grades are available, including water-dispersible types.
