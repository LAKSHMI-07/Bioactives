A healthy stew that doesn't use flour or butter.

Procedure

  1. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Slice the eggplant into rounds, boil the okra and cut it in half diagonally, and halve the onion and slice it thinly.
  3. Add olive oil to a frying pan and fry the chicken until golden brown. Remove from the frying pan and place on a plate.
  4. Add olive oil to a frying pan and fry the eggplant. When the eggplant is thoroughly coated with oil and soft, add the bell peppers and okra and quickly transfer to a frying plate.
  5. Add olive oil to a frying pan and fry the onion until it becomes soft. Add the pea protein and curry powder and fry further. Pour in the soup that you have dissolved the solid bouillon in beforehand little by little, add the chicken from step 3, cover and simmer for about 10 minutes.
  6. Once the chicken is cooked, add the cream and the vegetables (4) and simmer for about 5 minutes.

Remarks

Although it has a few steps, it can be easily made in one frying pan, so it is recommended when you don't have much of an appetite. It contains coconut oil to further boost your metabolism.
